About Us-Phoolbagan

"At The Rose, we blend the art of confectionary with the richness of multicuisine dining across our four Kolkata outlets.

Our Story

Gourmet Confectionary Excellence

The Rose stands out for its exceptional range of handcrafted desserts and confections, each meticulously crafted to offer a perfect blend of flavor and artistry.

Multicuisine Mastery:

Our menu showcases a diverse array of dishes from around the world, skillfully prepared to highlight the best of global flavors. Whether you're craving classic comfort foods or adventurous culinary experiences, The Rose offers a rich tapestry of multicuisine options to satisfy every palate.

Elegant Atmosphere Across Multiple Locations:

With four distinctive outlets across Kolkata, The Rose maintains a consistently sophisticated and inviting ambiance. Each location is designed to offer a unique dining experience, combining stylish decor with warm, attentive service to create the perfect setting for any occasion.

Opened in 1996, today, there are 3 restaurants in the North Kolkata area including Kankurgachi, Salt Lake & Laketown. If you’re searching for top-notch food, THE ROSE KITCHEN is unquestionably one of the spots to go to in Kolkata.

Our Specialties

The Rose is renowned for its exceptional specialties that set us apart. Our signature desserts are a highlight, featuring intricate pastries, decadent cakes, and artisanal confections that are as visually stunning as they are delicious. On the savory side, our multicuisine menu offers a range of standout dishes, from perfectly seasoned international classics to innovative creations that showcase the best of global flavors.
